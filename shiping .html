Shipping Policy

1. Digital Product Delivery

Since all our products are digital courses, no physical shipping is required. Once your payment is successfully processed, you will receive instant access to the course materials.

2. Delivery Method

  • Courses are delivered via a secure download link or login access on our website.
  • An email confirmation with access details will be sent to your registered email address.

3. Delivery Time

Access to your purchased course is typically available immediately after payment confirmation. In rare cases, delivery may take up to 2 hours due to technical reasons.

4. Non-Delivery Issues

If you do not receive access within 2 hours, please check your spam/junk folder or contact us at your-email@example.com. We will resolve the issue promptly.
